ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Eva. I live in London for five years and love the city's endless musical discoveries. This website is your ticket to exploring London through the tunes of local bands. Join me on a journey to iconic areas, where melodies bring each location to life.

ABOUT THIS WEBSITE

This project is open sourced on GitHub. I created this website out of love for music, but also to learn to embed external sources and to improve my coding skills.
